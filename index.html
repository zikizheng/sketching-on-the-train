Sketching on the Subway

This website is a collection of sketches that we have made to show the different perspectives on what we focus on when we sketch on the bts, in this case, there are people, objects, and setting.

The sketched objects are very interactable in different ways, you may click, hover, and drag them to see what they do and learn more about our observations on the train.
